Privacy Policy

Bouncing Orange · Fjordlight Games
Last updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fjordlight Games ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game Bouncing Orange (the "Game"). By playing the Game you agree to the practices described here.

We built the Game to need as little personal information as possible. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal identifying information. The following data is processed so the Game can function:

  • Game progress & settings — your level progress, coins, best scores, selected character, and audio settings are stored only on your device.
  • Leaderboard data — to show the online Endless leaderboard, we send a randomly generated player ID (created on your device, not linked to your real identity), a display name you choose, your selected character, and your best distance score to our leaderboard provider (SilentWolf). Please do not enter personal information as your display name.
  • Advertising data — our advertising provider (Google AdMob) may collect a mobile advertising identifier, device information, and usage data to serve and measure ads. See Section 3.
  • Purchases — in-app purchases (such as "Remove Ads" and character skins) are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To save and restore your game progress on your device.
  • To display the online leaderboard and your ranking.
  • To show advertisements and (if you choose) reward you for watching them.
  • To process in-app purchases and remove ads when purchased.
  • To diagnose problems and improve the Game.

3. Advertising & Your Choices

The Game shows ads through Google AdMob. Depending on your region and consent, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

  • Consent (EEA/UK): where required, we present Google's consent form so you can choose how your data is used for ads.
  • iOS App Tracking Transparency: you can allow or deny tracking when prompted, and change it any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Remove Ads: purchasing "Remove Ads" stops ads from showing.

Google's handling of advertising data is described in the Google Privacy Policy and AdMob policies.

4. Third-Party Services

The Game relies on these third parties, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policy
  • SilentWolf (leaderboard hosting) — silentwolf.com
  • Apple (app distribution & in-app purchases) — policy

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information (for example, as a leaderboard display name), contact us and we will remove it.

6. Data Retention

On-device data remains until you delete the app or clear its data. Leaderboard entries remain until they are overwritten by a newer score or removed on request. Advertising data is retained by Google according to its own policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete information associated with you. Because we identify leaderboard entries by a random device ID rather than your real identity, please contact us with details so we can locate and remove your entry on request.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Leaderboard data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
